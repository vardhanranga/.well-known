Travelling changed my life so much that I couldn’t imagine a life without it

When I started travelling, I was a timid, naive boy with no life experience and zero common sense. I had no sense of self-worth, didn’t have any confidence, had never been independent and didn’t know how to make friends. I suffered from debilitating anxiety and had panic attacks every few hours. I’d never been on a solo travel before. I didn’t know how to post a letter. Everyone thought I was crazy for leaving; nobody expected me to last..
